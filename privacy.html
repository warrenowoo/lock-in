Lock-In — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Lock-In is a personal health and fitness tracker. Your privacy is simple to explain because the app is built to keep everything on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Lock-In does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no analytics or tracking.

Where your data lives

Everything you log — water, food, workouts, creatine, to-dos, and your calendar history — is stored only on your device (in the app's local storage). It never leaves your phone and is never uploaded to any server.

Data deletion

Because your data is stored only on your device, deleting the app removes all of it. There is nothing stored elsewhere for us to delete.

Third parties

Lock-In does not use third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services, and does not send data to any third party.

Children

Lock-In does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach the developer via the project page at github.com/warrenowoo/lock-in.
