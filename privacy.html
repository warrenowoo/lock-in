Lock-In — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Lock-In is a personal health and fitness tracker. You can use it two ways, and your privacy depends on which you choose.

Using Lock-In without an account

If you don't sign in, everything stays on your device. Your water, food, workouts, creatine, to-dos, and calendar history are stored only in the app on your phone. Nothing is collected, transmitted, or shared.

Using Lock-In with an account

If you create an account (with email or Sign in with Apple) to sync your data and connect with friends, we store the following on our backend (hosted by Supabase) so those features can work:

  • Your email address (for your account).
  • Your username and display name (so friends can find you).
  • Your daily activity — a summary of your water, food, workouts, and creatine, plus the workout/meal posts that appear in your friends' feed.
  • Your friend connections and the reactions you send.

Who can see your data

Your activity is visible only to friends whose requests you have accepted, enforced by database security rules. We do not sell your data, show ads, use third-party analytics or trackers, or share your data with anyone for advertising or tracking.

Deleting your account and data

You can permanently delete your account at any time from within the app: Settings (gear icon) → Delete account. This immediately and permanently erases your account and all associated data (profile, activity, friends, and reactions) from our servers.

Children

Lock-In is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.
